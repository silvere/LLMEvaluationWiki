> arXiv: [2509.11101](https://arxiv.org/abs/2509.11101) | 2025-09-14 | Haokun Li, Yazhou Zhang, Jizhi Ding, Qiuchi Li, Peng Zhang

## 摘要

With the rapid advancement of Multimodal Large Language Models (MLLMs), they have demonstrated exceptional capabilities across a variety of vision-language tasks. However, current evaluation benchmarks predominantly focus on objective visual question answering or captioning, inadequately assessing the models' ability to understand complex and subjective human emotions.

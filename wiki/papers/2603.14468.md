# LongVidSearch: An Agentic Benchmark for Multi-hop Evidence Retrieval Planning in Long Videos

> arXiv: [2603.14468](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.14468) | 2026-03-15 | **第一作者**: Rongyi Yu | 全部作者: Rongyi Yu, Chenyuan Duan, Wentao Zhang

## 摘要

Long video question answering (Long-Video QA) increasingly relies on agentic tool use to retrieve evidence from long videos. In realistic settings, this process often requires multi-hop retrieval, where agents must iteratively gather multiple discontinuous evidence clips.

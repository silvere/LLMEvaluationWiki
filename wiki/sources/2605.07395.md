> 来源：arXiv preprint（2026）| 作者：Saloni Garg, Amit Sagtani

## 摘要

Efficient routing across multiple LLMs enables cost-quality tradeoffs by directing queries to the cheapest capable model. Prior work attributes routing headroom to an "unsolvability ceiling", queries no model in the pool can solve.

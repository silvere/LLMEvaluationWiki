## 摘要

Recent advancements in video models have shown tremendous progress, particularly in long video understanding. However, current benchmarks predominantly feature western-centric data and English as the dominant language, introducing significant biases in evaluation. To address this, we introduce MINERVA-Cultural, a challenging benchmark for multicultural and multilingual video reasoning.

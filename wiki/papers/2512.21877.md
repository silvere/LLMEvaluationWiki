# CricBench: A Multilingual Benchmark for Evaluating LLMs in Cricket Analytics

> arXiv: [2512.21877](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.21877) | 2025-12-26 | **第一作者**: Parth Agarwal | 全部作者: Parth Agarwal, Navya Kommuri, Trizal Garg, Prisha Singhal, Dhruv Shah, Vaibhav Devraj et al.

## 摘要

Cricket is the second most popular sport worldwide, with billions of fans seeking advanced statistical insights unavailable through standard web searches. Although LLMs have advanced significantly in Text-to-SQL tasks, their capability to handle domain-specific nuances and multilingual requirements in sports analytics remains under-explored. We present CricBench, a benchmark suite evaluating the intrinsic SQL generation abilities of LLMs on cricket data across four formats: Test, ODI, T20I, and ...

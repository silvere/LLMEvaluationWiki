# MaterialFigBENCH: benchmark dataset with figures for evaluating college-level materials science problem-solving abilities of multimodal large language models

> 来源：arXiv preprint（2026）| 作者：Michiko Yoshitake, Yuta Suzuki, Ryo Igarashi, Yoshitaka Ushiku, Keisuke Nagato

## 摘要

We present MaterialFigBench, a benchmark dataset designed to evaluate the ability of multimodal large language models (LLMs) to solve university-level materials science problems that require accurate interpretation of figures.

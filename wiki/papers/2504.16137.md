# Virology Capabilities Test (VCT): A Multimodal Virology Q&A Benchmark

> arXiv: [2504.16137](https://arxiv.org/abs/2504.16137) | 2025-04-21 | Jasper Götting, Pedro Medeiros, Jon G Sanders, Nathaniel Li, Long Phan, Karam Elabd et al.

## 摘要

We present the Virology Capabilities Test (VCT), a large language model (LLM) benchmark that measures the capability to troubleshoot complex virology laboratory protocols. Constructed from the inputs of dozens of PhD-level expert virologists, VCT consists of $322$ multimodal questions covering fundamental, tacit, and visual knowledge that is essential for practical work in virology laboratories.
